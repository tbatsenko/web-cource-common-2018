About

John is a UX Designer that works for a Fortune 500 company in Atlanta, GA. Ever since he was a child, he loved to make stuff on his own and show them to his parents, friends and classmates. Over the course of his childhood and throughout his school, he won numerous design prizes at various well known competitions across the United States and Canada. Due to his passion for design, he decided to pursue a Master’s degree in Human Computer Interaction and learn more about User Experience (UX) and how he can become a better designer.
